**From paper chaos to clean digital totals.**
Daily fuel sales tracking for PMS, AGO, Kerosene, and LPG.

## What It Does
- **Real-time Ledger:** Instant totals for pumps and attendants.
- **Reporting:** Quick-switch Daily, Weekly, and Monthly views.
- **Offline-First:** Data stays on your device via SQLite (WASM).
- **Lean Build:** Pure Vanilla JS. Zero bloat. Maximum speed.
- **Clean Experience:** No accounts. No servers. No ads.
