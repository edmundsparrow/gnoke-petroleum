Gnoke Petroleum · v1.0

⛽ Gnoke Petroleum

Offline-first daily sales tracker for fuel stations.

Track PMS, AGO, Kerosene and LPG across multiple pumps. View weekly and monthly breakdowns, name attendants per pump, and export to CSV — all without internet or an account.

Your data never leaves the device.
